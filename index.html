    💡 Quick Start Guide
    Your daily workflow in 6 steps
    1
    Pick your campaign. Use the Active Campaign selector at the bottom of the left menu. This drives your scripts, talk tracks, and quick answers.
    2
    Open Live Call Assist. Your opener, gatekeeper handling, discovery questions, and objection responses are all on one screen. Enter the lead info in the Current Lead panel before you dial.
    3
    Disposition every call. Tap a disposition button the moment the call ends. It auto-logs to your Activity Log and updates your counters. Never leave a call un-dispositioned.
    4
    Qualify live. When you reach a real conversation, click Qualify. Fill the Lead Qualification form as you talk — verify contact info, providers, systems, pain points, and interest. The form scores readiness automatically.
    5
    Build the handoff. At 75+ readiness the lead becomes QL/AE-FSE ready. Click Build Handoff Summary — it pulls everything (including your name and campaign) into a clean summary to copy into SAP CRM.
    6
    Clear your follow-ups. Check Home each morning for callbacks and follow-ups due. Overdue items show up on the manager's Coaching dashboard — stay ahead of them.
    Disposition Cheat Sheet
    No Answer Rang out, no contact.
    Left Message With Person Spoke to a person (gatekeeper/staff), left a message for the DM.
    Voicemail Left a voicemail.
    Email Sent Sent intro/follow-up email.
    Callback Set Scheduled a specific callback time.
    Not Interested Reached the right person, they declined.
    LinkedIn Outreach Sent a connection request or InMail.
    Webinar Invite Sent / Registered Webinar pipeline.
    On Site Visit Approved / Scheduled Rep on-site secured.
    Disqualified Not a fit.
    Bad Phone Number Number invalid/disconnected.
    Do Not Call Request Suppress — flag immediately.
    Meeting Set Your qualified lead — discovery meeting booked with the rep. Counts as a QL.
